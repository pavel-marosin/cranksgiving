2012 Results

Over 280 riders came out to Cranksgiving NYC 2012! We had only prepared for 250, and had only really expected 200 or so. This completely blew us away, and was by far the biggest Cranksgiving New York has ever seen. The results of the long ride and short ride are below. (Some of the manifests ended up getting lost, so there are some blank spaces. Sorry about that)

We estimate that if everyone spent about $15, that's over $4,200 worth of food that was donated. On top of that, our fundraiser brought in over $1,600 to City Harvest. Thank you to everyone who participated!
